🏆 Camera Phone Verdict

🥇 Xiaomi 17 Ultra Photography Purist

Get this if: you want the 1-inch sensor with real depth of field, better low light, and Leica's film-like color science. Thinner and lighter. The more "camera-first" device.

~€1,499 at MediaMarkt Gran Via 2, FNAC, or Xiaomi Store Passeig de Gràcia

🥈 Oppo Find X9 Ultra Zoom King

Get this if: you want 10x optical zoom (real telescope reach), dual dedicated telephoto lenses, and Hasselblad color. Better value at €1,200. First Oppo Ultra with full EU warranty and Google services.

~€1,200 — check FNAC, El Corte Inglés, or Amazon.es

Note: Vivo X300 Ultra (Forbes "best camera phone right now") is China-only. No European release, no Google services out of box, no warranty. Skip it unless you want the import headache via AliExpress/Wondamobile.
Both slaughter the S25 Ultra on camera hardware. Since you already daily an iPhone 17 Pro Max and S25 Ultra, this is a camera-first purchase — the Xiaomi 17 Ultra with its 1-inch Leica sensor is the purer choice.

Xiaomi 17 Ultra vs S25 Ultra

The Xiaomi 17 Ultra has a 1-inch sensor (vs S25 Ultra's 1/1.3") and Leica-tuned optics. The difference in depth of field, low light, and color rendering is significant — it's the closest a phone gets to a dedicated camera. Since you already have the iPhone 17 Pro Max and S25 Ultra, this is purely a camera upgrade.
