⚡What Is Chronicle?

Chronicle is a standalone, open-source investigation workbench that serves two passionate communities with one engine. Writers and game masters use it to build consistent fictional worlds. Security analysts use it to reconstruct attacks and find patterns. The AI doesn't just answer — it shows you how it got there.

🔧Core Features

Immutable Timeline

Append-only ledger of every AI thought, action, and user decision. Becomes the canonical truth — for fictional worlds or incident investigations. Cannot be erased, even by rogue agents.

Consensus: append-only memory

Split-Pane THINK / SAYS

Left pane shows the AI's step-by-step rationale — the real reasoning. Right pane shows the polished output. You see how it thinks, not just what it says. Builds genuine trust.

Consensus: THINK/SAYS split

Semantic Recall

Don't keyword-search your past. Ask "Show me similar character arcs" or "Find similar lateral movement patterns." AI surfaces relevant records by meaning, not text match.

Consensus: semantic retrieval

Multi-Session Persistence

Close your investigation and come back days or weeks later. Full context restored. Essential for year-long novels or 24/7 SOC rotations.

Consensus: multi-session persistence

Human-in-the-Loop Gates

Any canon-affecting change (lore edit) or network action requires explicit human approval. Recorded in the timeline. No accidental world-breaking or network damage.

Consensus: HITL approval gates

Multi-Model Deliberation

Run a "Writers' Room" or "Threat Brainstorming" where multiple AI models debate alternatives via OpenRouter. Curated, merged output — not a single model's guess.

Consensus + OpenRouter + Chimera

Row-Level Security

Invite co-authors or analysts with granular permissions. Each sees only their scope. Multi-tenant ready — one instance, many teams.

Consensus: row-level security

🎯Why Chronicle Wins

🕳️ Fable5 Gap

Fable5 just shut down. 40,000 creative writers are actively searching for alternatives right now. Chronicle launches as their new home with transparent AI, persistent worlds, and an open-source model.

🔍 Opaque Security AI

Splunk, CrowdStrike, Sentinel — none show the AI's reasoning. Chronicle's split-pane makes threat analysis trusted rather than blindly accepted. SOC teams adopt tools they can verify.

🔌 Plugin Marketplace

Community contributes "genre packs" (fantasy, cyberpunk) and "investigation packs" (ransomware response, Okta MFA bombing). Viral loop through shared playbooks. Every contribution strengthens the engine.

🧬 Pi Agent Pattern

Nobody needs to know Consensus exists. Chronicle is what people install. They love it, contribute plugins, find bugs, upstream fixes. The product drives the framework, not the other way around.

The insight: "I built a framework" gets zero users. "I built a tool that fixes your fragmented context, opaque AI, and lack of auditability" gets customers. Chronicle is the tool. Consensus is the engine nobody sees.
