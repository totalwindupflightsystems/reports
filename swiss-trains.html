🏆 Our Recommendation

Do the Bernina Express. It's the perfect weekend train — 4 hours, UNESCO route, and it starts in Tirano, Italy — just 2.5 hours from Milan.

Your weekend: Milan (Armani Silos + lunch) → Tirano → Bernina Express → St. Moritz (overnight) → explore the Engadin valley → back Sunday evening.

If you have more time, add the Gornergrat Bahn as a side trip from Zermatt — 33 minutes for the most insane Matterhorn view you'll ever see.
